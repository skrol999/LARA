About
"Music is how I survive, how I stay alive, and how I honor both who I am today and the child I once was."

LARA is a Stockholm-based artist and topliner recognized for her powerful vocals, sharp melodic instincts, and fearless creative vision. Having honed her craft through songwriting camps and studio sessions across the UK, Los Angeles, Sweden, and Korea, she has quickly established herself as a versatile and in-demand creative force.

Driven, self-aware, and fierce, LARA knows exactly who she is and what she stands for. She speaks directly to the young women of her generation, offering a space where vulnerability is strength and being weird is something to celebrate. She is building a singular sonic world that only she can carry.

"This project is my entire life translated into music — my feelings, thoughts, traumas, secrets, and growth. Like a quick dive into my personal diary. Through every song, I'm reclaiming space as someone who's always felt different, broken, or too much. I want my music to feel like a missing piece for people like me — like they can finally breathe and let their heart beat at the tempo it was meant to." — LARA

Live

On Stage

LARA is a driving force on stage. She started performing at a very young age, singing covers in bars and intimate venues — quickly building a reputation for a voice that stops a room. A multiple award winner during her school years, she's always known the stage was where she belonged. Today, her live sets translate the raw intimacy of her music into something visceral and unforgettable — every show a chance to make someone in the audience feel a little less alone.
